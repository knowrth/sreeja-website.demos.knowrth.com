About Us

This company, with a legacy of offering finest home interiors in India for the past 10 years, is the choice of a growing number of clients. Sreeja Interiors combines innovation, freshness and life into interior designing and execution.

With a telling presence as the best interior designers in Andhra Pradesh we have completed more than 100+ projects in various locations across AP till date. We design, produce and implement premium home interiors to the satisfaction of clients.

Our journey began with a vision to bring craftsmanship, innovation, and timeless design to Indian homes. Today, we stand as one of the fastest-growing and most trusted names in Andhra Pradesh.
