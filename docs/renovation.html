Renovations

Renovation

We involve improving, repairing, or adding to a property to enhance its functionality, aesthetics, or value, and can range from minor cosmetic updates like painting to major structural changes. Common renovation projects include kitchens, bathrooms, and basement remodelling, as well as exterior enhancements. To begin, plan the scope of the project, which may involve structural repairs, system upgrades (electrical, plumbing), window and door replacements, interior finishes, and final inspections.

Glass Works

Involves the creation, installation, and repair of products using glass as the primary material. Depending on the specialization, this can range from installing and replacing windows in buildings to creating decorative art pieces. Skilled individuals, known as glass workers or glaziers, use tools to measure, cut, and shape glass, ensuring it's securely fitted into frames or structures.

Electrical

We do Installing, altering, maintaining, and repairing electrical systems, components, and equipment to ensure safe and efficient operation in residential, commercial, and industrial settings. This work involves interpreting blueprints, running wiring and conduits, installing fixtures and devices, diagnosing power issues, and ensuring all work complies with electrical codes and regulations.

Plumbing

Installing, repairing, and maintaining pipes, fixtures, and systems that transport water, gas, and waste in residential, commercial, and industrial settings. Key tasks include installing items like sinks, toilets, and appliances, as well as repairing leaks, blockages, and malfunctioning equipment. Plumbers also work with drainage, heating, and gas systems, requiring technical skills, (e.g., sinks, toilets, showers), appliances (e.g., dishwashers), and entire water, gas, or drainage systems.

Wallpapers

A decorative and often utilitarian covering for interior walls, made from patterned paper or other materials, designed to enhance or transform the aesthetic of a room. It comes in various forms, such as paper-based, vinyl-coated, or non-woven, and can feature designs ranging from traditional patterns like damask to modern geometric prints or even personalized murals. Homeowners select wallpaper to create a specific ambiance, from a classic and opulent feel to a modern and minimalistic look, or to simply unify a room's decor.
